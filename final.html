Evan Engelhardt's Final Project

This is my final project for CSC106. It is a version of rock, paper, scissors where the user competes against a randomized computer in a best of three tournament.
Instructions: press the start button. Once your on the game screen select either rock, paper, or scissors with their labeled buttons to play against the computer. In both cases if you win or lose you press the home button on the win or loss screen to go home. The home button on the game screen also takes you back to the start.
